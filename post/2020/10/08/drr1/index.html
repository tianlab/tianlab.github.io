【论坛】10-08 19:00 Building African Youth Capacity in DRR.

2020-10-08

  • Time： 2020-10-8 19:00-21:00

OBJECTIVES

As the International Day for Disaster Risk Reduction draws closer, AYAB DRR is teaming up with experts and stakeholders in the field to discuss opportunities and challenges for building capacity among African Youth in the Disaster Risk Reduction.

OUTLINE

African Youth Advisory Board on Disaster Risk Reduction (AYAB DRR) will be hosting a short webinar exploring the topic of Building capacity among African Youth particularly in the field of Disaster Risk Reduction. Colleagues from UNDRR, PERIPERI U, Stellenbosch University, the African Youth ENVOY and several other will lead discussions on the topic, and be available to answer and discuss queries from attendees.

Schedule

The Webinar will be hosted on Thursday 8 October 2020 at 11h00 GMT via Microsoft teams live event (please find the link here: https://tinyurl.com/y3sdkj8d)

Please feel free to share among your staff, students, colleagues and any other stakeholders you feel would like to engage with us on this topic.

Kind regards,

Alberto and the AYAB DRR Team
