Sections, Categories and Tags of XRLAB site

2021-04-16

Sections are meant for the folders of your posts. Think of these as physical location of the posts. Sections are hierarchical which means you can create sub-sections.

Categories are meant for broad grouping of your posts. Think of these as general topics or the table of contents for your site. Categories are hierarchical which means you can create sub-categories.

Tags are meant to describe specific details of your posts. Think of these as your site’s index words. They let you micro-categorize your content. Tags are not hierarchical.
